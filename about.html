Why Positioning Matters: The Referral Dependency Problem

Most fractional executives rely 100% on referrals to grow their business. While referrals are great, they are also dangerous because they are outside of your control. When referrals slow down, your business enters a "feast or famine" cycle.

Strategic Authority Positioning solves this by creating an inbound system. Instead of waiting for someone to mention your name, you are consistently appearing in front of your ideal clients as the obvious solution to their problems.
