Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

At Muhammad Ali | LinkedIn Authority Architect, reachable through this website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by our systems and how we use it.

1. General Information

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via email at aliofficial.busi@gmail.com.

2. Log Files

Muhammad Ali | Executive Authority Architect follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this as part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Muhammad Ali | Executive Authority Architect uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to https://aliofficialpk.github.io/aasfore-authority-architect/ and other sites on the internet.

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.
