The Executive Authority Engine™ Framework

A systematic, 5-pillar approach to building a LinkedIn presence that attracts high-value strategic partnerships.

Moving Beyond Random Acts of Content

Most executives approach LinkedIn with "random acts of content." They post when they have time, engage sporadically, and wonder why it doesn't lead to business. My framework replaces this chaos with a structured engine that works for you, even when you aren't active.

PILLAR 01

Authority Positioning Audit

Description: A deep dive into your current market standing, niche clarity, and competitor positioning. We find the "white space" you can own.

Proposed Outcome: A clear, documented Authority Map that defines exactly what you stand for and who you serve.

PILLAR 02

Profile Optimization for Conversion

Description: We transform your profile from a passive resume into an active sales page. We rewrite your headline, about section, and featured items to drive specific actions.

Who it's for: Professionals getting profile views but no inbound messages or connection requests from target clients.

Problem Solved: Low conversion rates and "Resume-style" positioning.

Process: Multi-pass copy rewrite, layout restructuring, and high-impact CTA placement.

Expected Outcome: A profile that immediately establishes trust and prompts high-value prospects to engage.

PILLAR 03

Executive-Level Content Architecture

Description: A sustainable system for creating high-authority content without spending 10 hours a week writing. We build content "pillars" based on your insights.

Who it's for: Busy executives who know they should post but don't have the time or a structured plan.

Problem Solved: Inconsistent posting and "content fatigue."

Process: Development of a 30-day rolling content calendar based on your specific Authority Map.

Expected Outcome: A predictable flow of high-authority content that builds your reputation as a thought leader automatically.

Description: A proprietary method where I interview you to extract your unique professional experiences and turn them into viral-ready strategic insights.

Inbound Conversion Optimization
